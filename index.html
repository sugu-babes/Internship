About Us

Grow Your Business With Makers Mind

Makers Mind is an Indian Company that deals with Information Technology (IT). Makers Mind is specialized at Web Development, Digital Marketing, App Development, Gaming and Augmented Reality(AR).Our well-established company in Visakhapatnam is highly equipped with the expertise and capabilities of planning, development, delivery and support for huge projects in different domains. Our team is made up of talented and passionate young people who work together to achieve the best results.

We live by the motto, "Client satisfaction should be our top priority", and we will always stick to this motto.We are dedicated, hardworking, and disciplined towards our projects which makes clients work with us more often. We implement innovative ideas and strategies to meet client needs and deliver the best results. Globally, we work on multiple projects that are related to app development, web development, and digital marketing.
